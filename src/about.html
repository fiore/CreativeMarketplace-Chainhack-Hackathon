Our Goal

Our goal is to empower individuals to share in the discovery of new artists and invest in the ones they believe in. The investment and promotion of these new artists will allow investors to reap financial benefit in the future potential of the artist as their reputation increases.

Why Blockchain?

Transactional Trust + Decentralized Network + Transparency

Blockchain will allow a decentralized avenue for people to challenge the current perceived bias of the industry which belongs in the hands of the select few (e.g. record labels, publishing houses, etc.)

The up and coming artist will now have a platform for exposure.

Our vision is to allow passionate individuals (artists or followers) be rewarded for their contributions and talent.

In an ideal world, the intellectual property rights would be tokenized for each artist and a royalty-based method would be implemented.

Why Ethereum + Solidity?

Because the focus of the project is on utility token, the back-end was programmed with Solidity. Smart contracts allow us the ability to set pre-defined rules for transactions between followers and artists which helps to enforce compliance for token redemption and transfers. The users will be able to purchase ERC20 Tokens that are minted and can be invested into the artist.
